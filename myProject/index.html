Some encouragement from an egg, a potato, and a tea leaf

Imagine that an egg, a potato, and a tea leaf are each cooked in pots of boiling water.

After some time, they are taken out. The egg, soft and fragile before, is now solid and firm. The potato, hard as a rock before, is now soft and mushy. And the tea leaf changed the water from clear to a sage green.

Each item was placed into the same conditions - boiling water - but each had a different transformation.

So...if you are ever feeling down for not having same results as everyone else, do not be! You have unique qualities to use to your advantage :)
